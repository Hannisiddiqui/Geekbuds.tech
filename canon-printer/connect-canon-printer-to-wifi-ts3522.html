How To Connect Canon Printer To Wifi TS3522: Step-by-Step Guide

Struggling with how to connect Canon printer to WiFi TS3522? Don’t worry—you’re not alone! Many users find it challenging to set up their printers wirelessly, but this guide will walk you through the process in simple, easy-to-follow steps. Whether you’re using a computer, mobile phone, or tablet, this tutorial will help you master the Canon TS3522 WiFi connection in no time.

By the end of this guide, you’ll have your Canon PIXMA TS3522 ready for wireless printing from anywhere in your home or office. Let’s dive into the setup so you can enjoy the full benefits of Canon TS3522 wireless printing without the hassle!

Why Choose Wireless Printing for Your Canon TS3522?

Wireless printing offers multiple benefits, especially when using a device as versatile as the Canon PIXMA TS3522. Let’s explore why you should prioritize how to connect Canon printer to WiFi TS3522:

  • Freedom to Print from Anywhere: No more dealing with cables or staying close to your printer. You can print from any device connected to the same WiFi network, whether it’s a computer in the office or a mobile phone in the living room.
  • Multiple Device Connection: After successfully completing your Canon TS3522 network setup, you can print from various devices like laptops, tablets, and smartphones without the need to reconnect each time.
  • Productivity Boost: Once you’ve figured out how to connect Canon printer to WiFi TS3522, you’ll find that wireless printing streamlines your workflow, saves time, and reduces the clutter of cables.

Preparation Before You Connect Your Canon TS3522 Printer to WiFi

Before you jump into the setup, ensure you have everything you need to make the process of how to connect Canon printer to WiFi TS3522 as smooth as possible:

  • WiFi Network Details: Make sure you know your WiFi name (SSID) and password. You’ll need these during the setup process.
  • Turn on Your Canon TS3522: Ensure your printer is powered on and ready for setup.
  • Driver Installation: Download the latest drivers for your Canon PIXMA TS3522 from Canon’s official site to avoid compatibility issues.
  • Stable Internet Connection: A strong and stable WiFi signal will make it easier to complete the Canon TS3522 wireless setup without interruptions.

Step-by-Step Guide on How to Connect Canon Printer to WiFi TS3522

A. Connecting Canon TS3522 to WiFi on Windows

  1. Power on your Canon TS3522 printer and press the WiFi button until the blue WiFi light flashes.
  2. On your Windows device, connect to your home WiFi network.
  3. Download and install the printer drivers from Canon’s website (ij.start.canon).
  4. During the driver installation, select "Wireless Setup" and enter your WiFi credentials.
  5. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the Canon TS3522 WiFi connection.
  6. Once the connection is established, print a test page to verify the connection.

B. Connecting Canon TS3522 to WiFi on macOS

  1. Turn on your printer and press the WiFi button until the light starts blinking.
  2. Go to System Preferences > Printers & Scanners on your Mac.
  3. Download the latest driver for macOS from Canon’s official website.
  4. Click on the "+" symbol to add your Canon TS3522 to the printer list.
  5. Select your wireless network and enter your password to complete the Canon TS3522 network setup.
  6. Print a test page to confirm that your printer is now connected to the WiFi.

C. Connecting Canon TS3522 to WiFi Using Mobile Devices (Android/iOS)

  1. Download the Canon PRINT Inkjet/SELPHY app from the Google Play Store or the Apple App Store.
  2. Open the app, tap "Add Printer," and select Canon TS3522.
  3. Press the WiFi button on your printer until the WiFi icon flashes, indicating that it’s in pairing mode.
  4. The app will detect your printer and prompt you to select your WiFi network.
  5. Enter your WiFi password and complete the setup process.

Troubleshooting Common Issues During Canon TS3522 WiFi Setup

If you encounter any difficulties during the Canon TS3522 wireless setup, here are a few common problems and their solutions:

  • Issue: The printer isn’t appearing on the network. Solution: Make sure your printer and the device are on the same WiFi network. Reboot both devices if necessary.
  • Issue: WiFi signal is too weak. Solution: Move the printer closer to the router and ensure there are no obstructions.
  • Issue: Incorrect WiFi password entered. Solution: Double-check the password and try re-entering it during the setup process.

Optimizing Print Quality for Canon TS3522

Once your printer is successfully connected, you can improve print quality with a few tips:

  • Use High-Quality Ink Cartridges: Stick to genuine Canon ink cartridges to maintain high-quality output.
  • Adjust Print Settings: For different types of documents, adjust your settings. Photos may require photo paper and high-resolution settings, while documents can use regular paper.
  • Clean Printheads Regularly: Regular maintenance will prevent streaks and smudges, ensuring your Canon TS3522 wireless printing remains flawless.

Conclusion
