How to Connect Canon PIXMA TS3522 Printer to WiFi: Complete Guide

Connecting your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to WiFi is an essential step to unlock the convenience of wireless printing. Whether you're using a laptop, desktop, or mobile device, learning how to connect Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to WiFi is simple and quick. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll show you the exact steps to set up your Canon printer wirelessly, making it easier to print from anywhere within your home or office.

By the end of this guide, you’ll be able to seamlessly use your Canon TS3522 wireless connection, troubleshoot any issues that arise, and optimize your printer for the best performance. We’ll cover different connection methods, including for Windows, macOS, and mobile devices, so no matter what system you use, this guide on how to connect Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to WiFi will help you set it up.

Before You Begin: Essential Preparations

Before you start the Canon PIXMA TS3522 WiFi setup, it’s crucial to make sure everything is ready for the process. Here’s what you need to prepare:

  • Printer Readiness: Ensure your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer is turned on, and the ink cartridges are properly installed. Load paper into the tray for test prints during the setup process.
  • WiFi Credentials: Have your WiFi network name (SSID) and password handy as you'll need them to connect your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to WiFi.
  • Download Printer Drivers: Go to the Canon website to download the latest drivers for your printer. This step is essential to ensure that your computer communicates effectively with the Canon TS3522 wireless connection.

How to Connect Canon PIXMA TS3522 to WiFi Using a Laptop or Computer

Steps for Windows Users

  1. Download Drivers: Head to the Canon website and download the printer drivers specific to your Canon PIXMA TS3522 WiFi setup.
  2. Initiate Setup: Turn on your Canon TS3522 printer and press the Wireless Connect button on the printer until the WiFi light begins to blink. This indicates the printer is ready to be connected to the WiFi network.
  3. Run the Setup Program: Install the driver software on your Windows PC and follow the on-screen instructions. Choose the "WiFi Setup" option when prompted to connect your Canon TS3522 printer to WiFi.
  4. Connect to WiFi: Select your WiFi network from the list that appears, then enter your WiFi password to complete the Canon PIXMA TS3522 WiFi setup.
  5. Test Print: Once the connection is successful, perform a test print to ensure your Canon TS3522 wireless connection is functioning properly.

Steps for Mac Users

  1. Download Drivers: Download the appropriate drivers for macOS from Canon's website to ensure compatibility for your Canon PIXMA TS3522 WiFi setup.
  2. Wireless Connect: Press and hold the Wireless Connect button on the printer until the WiFi light starts blinking. Your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer is now ready to connect to your WiFi network.
  3. Mac Setup: Open the downloaded setup file on your Mac and follow the installation steps. When prompted, connect your Canon TS3522 printer to WiFi by selecting your network and entering the password.
  4. Add Printer: Go to System Preferences > Printers & Scanners, click the "+" button, and select your Canon PIXMA TS3522 from the available list of printers.

How to Connect Canon PIXMA TS3522 to WiFi Using a Mobile Device

For iOS Users:

  1. Download the Canon PRINT App: Download the Canon PRINT Inkjet/SELPHY app from the Apple App Store to enable WiFi printing directly from your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Connect to Printer: Launch the app, select “Add Printer,” and follow the in-app instructions to connect your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to WiFi. Ensure that your printer and mobile device are on the same WiFi network.
  3. Print Wirelessly: After successfully connecting, you can use AirPrint to print directly from any iOS device using your Canon TS3522 wireless connection.

For Android Users:

  1. Download the Canon PRINT App: Install the Canon PRINT Inkjet/SELPHY app from Google Play Store to manage the WiFi setup for your printer.
  2. Wireless Setup: Open the app, and follow the prompts to connect your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to the network.
  3. Start Printing: Once connected, you can print directly from your Android device using the Canon TS3522 wireless connection for a hassle-free experience.

Using the WPS Button for Quick WiFi Setup

If your router supports WPS (Wi-Fi Protected Setup), you can use this method for a faster way to connect your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to WiFi. Here's how:

  1. Activate WPS on Printer: Press and hold the WiFi button on your printer until the power light flashes.
  2. Press WPS Button on Router: Within two minutes, press the WPS button on your router to automatically connect the printer to your WiFi network.
  3. Connection Confirmation: The WiFi light on your printer will stop flashing and remain steady once the connection is successful. Your Canon PIXMA TS3522 WiFi setup is now complete.

Troubleshooting Common WiFi Setup Issues

Despite following the steps, sometimes you may encounter issues when trying to connect your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer to WiFi. Below are some common issues and solutions:

  • Problem: The printer is not connecting to the WiFi network. Solution: Ensure the printer is within range of your WiFi router and that the correct network credentials are entered. Restart the router and printer if needed.
  • Problem: The printer cannot be found on the network. Solution: Check if the WiFi light on the printer is solid. If not, press the Wireless Connect button again to restart the setup process.

Maintaining Your Canon PIXMA TS3522 for Optimal Performance

To keep your Canon PIXMA TS3522 printer running smoothly, it's important to perform regular maintenance and updates:

  • Firmware Updates: Regularly check for firmware updates on the Canon website to ensure your printer remains compatible with new devices and networks.
  • Regular Cleaning: Clean the printhead regularly to avoid clogged nozzles, ensuring the best possible print quality.
  • Using Genuine Ink: Always use genuine Canon ink cartridges to maintain optimal performance and print quality.
