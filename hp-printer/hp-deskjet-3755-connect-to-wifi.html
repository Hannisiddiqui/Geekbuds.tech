How HP DeskJet 3755 Connect to WiFi: A Complete Step-by-Step Guide

Setting up wireless printing for your HP DeskJet 3755 can significantly enhance your overall printing experience, allowing you to print from various devices like smartphones, tablets, and laptops—all without the need for cables. If you're wondering how to connect your HP DeskJet 3755 to WiFi, you've come to the right place. This guide will walk you through the process of setting up the HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi quickly and efficiently.

Whether you're using the HP Smart App, WPS, or manual connection methods, this detailed guide will ensure that your HP DeskJet 3755 WiFi installation is smooth and hassle-free. By following these steps, you will easily achieve a seamless HP 3755 wireless setup in no time, providing the freedom to print from anywhere in your home or office.

Unboxing and Initial Setup

Before you start the HP DeskJet 3755 WiFi connection, make sure your printer is properly unboxed and set up for operation.

Unbox Your HP DeskJet 3755

  • Remove Packaging: Take the HP DeskJet 3755 out of its box and remove all tapes and packaging materials carefully.
  • Connect to Power: Plug in the power cable to a nearby outlet and connect the other end to the printer. Turn on the printer by pressing the power button.
  • Install Ink Cartridges: Open the ink cartridge access door, insert the provided ink cartridges, and close the door securely.
  • Load Paper: Add plain white paper to the input tray so your printer is ready for action.

Once your printer is fully set up, you’re ready to proceed with the HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi steps.

Connecting the HP DeskJet 3755 to WiFi

Now, let's explore the different methods to connect your HP DeskJet 3755 to WiFi. Each method offers a simple and effective way to set up your wireless connection, allowing you to enjoy the convenience of cable-free printing.

Using HP Auto Wireless Connect

One of the easiest ways to achieve an HP 3755 WiFi connection is through the HP Auto Wireless Connect feature. Here’s how to set it up:

  1. Enable WiFi on Your Printer: Press and hold the WiFi button on your HP DeskJet 3755 until the WiFi light begins to blink.
  2. Download the HP Smart App: Go to the HP website or your app store and download the HP Smart App on your smartphone, tablet, or computer.
  3. Launch the App: Open the HP Smart App and follow the on-screen instructions. The app will automatically detect your printer.
  4. Connect to WiFi: The app will guide you through the necessary steps to connect the printer to your home WiFi network, making the HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi setup a breeze.

After completing these steps, your HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi process will be successful, and you can print wirelessly from any device on the network.

Using WPS (WiFi Protected Setup)

If your router supports WPS (WiFi Protected Setup), it’s another easy method to get your HP DeskJet 3755 WiFi installation done quickly:

  1. Press the WPS Button on Your Router: Locate the WPS button on your router and press it.
  2. Press the WPS Button on Your Printer: Within two minutes of pressing the router’s WPS button, press the WPS button on the HP DeskJet 3755 printer.

Once both buttons have been pressed, the printer will automatically establish a connection to your WiFi network, completing the HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi process without needing to input a password.

Manual WiFi Connection

For those who prefer more control or don't have WPS available, you can still complete the HP 3755 WiFi connection manually:

  1. Open Wireless Setup Wizard: Navigate through the printer's control panel and open the Wireless Setup Wizard.
  2. Choose Your Network: From the list of available WiFi networks, select your network.
  3. Enter the Password: Input your WiFi password when prompted and wait for the connection to establish.

Once your HP DeskJet 3755 is connected to your WiFi network, you’re all set to begin printing wirelessly.

Installing the Printer Software

Once the HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi is completed, it's time to install the printer software to ensure smooth operation.

  1. Visit HP’s Website: Go to 123.hp.com/setup and enter your printer model (HP DeskJet 3755).
  2. Download the Software: Download the necessary drivers and software for your operating system (Windows, macOS, etc.).
  3. Install the Software: Run the installer and follow the prompts to complete the HP DeskJet 3755 WiFi installation process.

After installing the software, your HP DeskJet 3755 wireless setup will be fully operational, allowing you to print wirelessly with ease.

Troubleshooting Common WiFi Connection Issues

Occasionally, you may run into issues with the HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi process. Here are common problems and their solutions.

HP Printer Offline

If your HP DeskJet 3755 appears as offline, check the following:

  • Ensure the printer and your device are connected to the same WiFi network.
  • Restart your printer, router, and the device you're trying to print from.
  • Re-run the HP DeskJet 3755 connect to WiFi steps if necessary.

Incorrect WiFi Password

If you're having trouble connecting due to a password prompt:

  • Double-check that you are entering the correct WiFi password.
  • Ensure that no spaces or typos are present in the password field during the

Slow WiFi Connectivity

For slow wireless printing:

  • Move your printer closer to your WiFi router to improve the signal strength.
  • Restart your router to refresh the network connection and boost performance.
