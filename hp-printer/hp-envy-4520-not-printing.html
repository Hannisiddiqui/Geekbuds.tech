Fixing HP Envy 4520 Not Printing: A Complete Troubleshooting Guide

If your HP Envy 4520 not printing, you're likely experiencing one of the most common printer issues that can halt productivity. Whether you're trying to print an important document or just need a quick page for work, it can be incredibly frustrating when the printer doesn’t respond. Why isn’t my HP Envy 4520 printing? This problem can occur for various reasons, from software glitches to wireless connection issues. In this comprehensive guide, we will walk you through all the potential causes and solutions for fixing HP Envy 4520 not printing errors. By following our step-by-step troubleshooting process, you will be able to resolve printer offline issues, fix outdated drivers, and address wireless printing issues, among other common problems.

Let’s dive in and explore how to get your HP Envy 4520 printing again with minimal hassle.

Common Reasons Why HP Envy 4520 May Not Be Printing

There are multiple reasons why your HP Envy 4520 is not printing. Below are some common issues to consider:

  1. Printer Offline Status: One of the most frequent problems causing your HP Envy 4520 not responding is when it is set to offline. This happens when the printer is not properly connected to your computer or network. It can be easily overlooked, but it's a critical factor.
  2. Outdated or Missing Drivers: An outdated or missing driver can disrupt the communication between your HP Envy 4520 and your computer, making it impossible for the printer to work. If the drivers are corrupted or incompatible, you will experience HP Envy 4520 not printing errors.
  3. Wireless Connectivity Problems: Wireless printing issues are another common culprit for non-printing problems. If your printer isn’t connected to the right Wi-Fi network or has a weak signal, the connection between your computer and printer will be interrupted.
  4. Print Queue Errors: The print queue can become clogged with old or stuck jobs, preventing any new documents from being printed. This can be a frustrating issue to troubleshoot, but it's usually easy to clear up.
  5. Ink Cartridge Recognition Issues: Sometimes, the HP Envy 4520 not printing is due to issues with the ink cartridges, such as poor contact with the printer, expired cartridges, or damaged parts.

Step-by-Step Solutions to Fix HP Envy 4520 Not Printing

Let’s look at the solutions to the common issues preventing your HP Envy 4520 from printing.

Resolving Printer Offline Issues

If your printer is offline, follow these steps to get it back online:

  1. Check Printer Status: Ensure that the printer is powered on and that there are no error messages on the printer display. Sometimes a simple restart is all it takes.
  2. Set Printer Online:
    • Windows: Go to Control Panel > Devices and Printers. Right-click on the HP Envy 4520 and select See what’s printing. Uncheck Use Printer Offline from the printer menu.
    • Mac: Go to System Preferences > Printers & Scanners, select your printer, and ensure it is set to Default Printer.
  3. Reconnect the Printer to the Network: If your printer is connected wirelessly, ensure it is connected to the same Wi-Fi network as your computer. You can do this through the printer’s Wireless Setup Wizard.

Fixing Driver and Software Issues

Outdated or corrupted drivers often lead to HP Envy 4520 not printing errors. Here's how to fix them:

  1. Update Printer Drivers: Head to the HP Support Website and download the latest drivers for the HP Envy 4520. After installing the latest version, restart your computer to check if the issue is resolved.
  2. Reinstall Printer Drivers: If the printer drivers are still causing issues, you might need to uninstall and reinstall them. To do this, go to Devices and Printers, right-click your printer, and select Remove Device. Then, reinstall the drivers from HP's official website.

Addressing Wireless Printing Problems

Wireless issues can easily prevent the HP Envy 4520 from printing. Follow these steps to troubleshoot:

  1. Verify Wi-Fi Connection: Ensure your printer is connected to the correct Wi-Fi network. If your router is experiencing any issues, try restarting it and reconnecting the printer to the network.
  2. Reconfigure Printer’s Wireless Settings: Access the printer’s control panel, select Wireless Settings > Wireless Setup Wizard, and reconnect the printer to the network.
  3. Restart Printer and Router: Sometimes, simply restarting both the printer and router can resolve wireless connectivity issues.

Resetting HP Envy 4520

If none of the above fixes work, performing a reset on your HP Envy 4520 can be a helpful last resort:

  1. Factory Reset: Turn off the printer. Press and hold the Cancel and Wireless buttons at the same time for about 10 seconds to reset the printer.
  2. Reconfigure Printer: After resetting, you will need to reconnect the printer to your network and reset any custom settings you had previously configured.

Handling Print Queue and Spooler Errors

Print queue and spooler errors often block printing tasks. Here’s how to resolve them:

  1. Clear the Print Queue:
    • On Windows: Right-click your printer under Devices and Printers and select See what’s printing. Cancel all pending print jobs.
    • On Mac: Go to Printers & Scanners, select your printer, and clear the queue.
  2. Restart the Print Spooler Service:
    • Windows: Press Windows + R, type services.msc, and press Enter. Find Print Spooler, right-click, and select Restart.

Ink Cartridge Troubleshooting

If your HP Envy 4520 not printing due to ink cartridge issues, follow these steps:

  1. Check Cartridge Installation: Ensure that the cartridges are correctly installed and seated. A loose cartridge can often cause problems.
  2. Clean the Cartridge Contacts: Clean the contacts of the ink cartridges with a lint-free cloth to remove any dust or residue.
  3. Replace Ink Cartridges: If the cartridges are old or damaged, replace them with new ones.

Expert Tips for Maintaining the HP Envy 4520 Printer

Proper maintenance can help avoid issues like HP Envy 4520 not printing in the future:

  1. Keep Software Up-to-Date: Regularly check for driver updates on HP’s website to avoid software-related issues.
  2. Store Ink Cartridges Properly: Ensure your cartridges are stored in a cool, dry place.
  3. Use Genuine Cartridges: Always use original HP ink cartridges to prevent recognition issues and ensure consistent performance.

In conclusion, troubleshooting HP Envy 4520 not printing involves checking common issues like printer offline status, outdated drivers, wireless connectivity, and ink cartridge problems.
